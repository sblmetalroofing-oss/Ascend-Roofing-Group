How often should I have my roof inspected?

In Australia, due to the harsh weather conditions (intense sun, heavy rain, or strong winds), we recommend:

  • Annual inspections to catch any early signs of damage or wear.
  • Post-storm inspections to check for any immediate damage caused by extreme weather events.

Regular maintenance can extend the life of your roof and prevent costly repairs down the line.

How can I tell if my roof needs repair or replacement?

Signs your roof may need repair or replacement include:

  • Leaks or water stains inside your home
  • Missing, cracked, or broken roof tiles (if applicable)
  • Rusted or corroded metal roofing
  • Sagging rooflines
  • Blocked gutters or debris buildup
  • Higher energy bills (due to insulation issues)

If you see any of these signs, contact us for a professional assessment.

Is council approval required for roofing work in Australia?

Generally, roof repairs and restorations don't need council approval. However, significant structural changes like full roof replacements or extensions might require it. We can help with advice and paperwork if needed.

What is the cost of a roof replacement in Australia?

The cost of a roof replacement depends on several factors:

  • Size and pitch of your roof
  • Type of materials used (e.g., Colorbond, Trimdek, Kliplok, Custom orb)
  • Labour costs
  • Any structural repairs needed

We provide free quotes, so you'll know the exact cost before we start.

To get your roof ready for storm season:

  • Clean gutters and downpipes for proper water flow.
  • Check for loose or damaged tiles or sheets.
  • Have a professional inspection before storm season to address potential issues.
