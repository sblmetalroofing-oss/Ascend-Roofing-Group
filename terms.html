Acceptance of Orders

Ascend Roofing Group Pty Ltd will only be bound by orders that have been formally accepted in writing.

Quotation Validity

All quotes provided are valid for a period of sixty (60) days. Our terms of trade require payment within 14 days of invoicing. Quotes are based on N3 wind loading specifications. All of our roofing quotes are classed as our contracts, and once agreed/accepted, they become a binding agreement between ourselves and the client.

Installation Notice

To schedule your installation, please provide a minimum notice of 1 to 4 weeks prior to the site measurement. This allows Ascend Roofing Group adequate time to arrange contractors and scaffolding. Our quotations are based on the completion of work as outlined in the scope. If site conditions necessitate a change in the work plan, we reserve the right to reassess our pricing.

Safety Requirements

Safety battens are mandatory for rafters exceeding 600mm.

Exclusions

Please note that our quotes do not include Hoods, Skylights, Brick Flashings, or Rainwater Heads; however, these can be arranged upon request.

Access and Safety

It is essential to ensure adequate access to the job site. Insufficient access may lead to delays. Scaffolding and edge protection are required for any work conducted at heights above 2 meters, roofs with a pitch greater than 26 degrees, or within 1.5 meters from a boundary. These will be provided by the builder or client unless otherwise specified in the quote.
